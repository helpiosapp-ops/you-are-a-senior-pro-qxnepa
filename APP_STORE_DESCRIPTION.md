
# ExitPlanner - App Store Listing

## App Name

## Subtitle

## Description

**Plan your career exit with confidence and calm.**

ExitPlanner is a privacy-first app designed to help professionals thoughtfully prepare for career transitions—whether you're planning a resignation, preparing for potential layoffs, negotiating an exit, or taking a career break.

**What ExitPlanner Offers:**

✓ **Guided Preparation Checklists**
Step-by-step guidance for four exit scenarios with comprehensive preparation items covering legal, financial, and practical considerations.

✓ **Common Mistakes to Avoid**
Learn from others' experiences with curated lists of pitfalls to avoid for each exit path.

✓ **Key Considerations**
Important factors to think about before making your move, tailored to your specific situation.

✓ **Personal Notes**
Private space to organize your thoughts, questions, and reminders.

✓ **Exportable Plans**
Generate clean, professional PDF documents of your exit plan to keep for your records.

✓ **Complete Privacy**
All data stored locally on your device. No cloud sync, no tracking, no accounts required.

**Four Exit Paths:**

1. **Resignation** - Planning to leave on your own terms
2. **Layoff Risk** - Preparing for potential job loss
3. **Negotiated Exit** - Discussing mutual separation
4. **Career Break** - Taking intentional time away

**Privacy & Security:**

- No registration or login required
- All data stored locally on your device
- No cloud synchronization
- No analytics or tracking
- No ads or in-app purchases
- Your information never leaves your device

**Important Disclaimer:**

ExitPlanner provides informational guidance only. It does not constitute legal, financial, or professional advice. Always consult qualified professionals for your specific situation.

**Perfect For:**

- Professionals planning career changes
- Employees preparing for potential layoffs
- Anyone considering a negotiated exit
- People planning career breaks or sabbaticals

Take control of your career transition with calm, organized preparation. Download ExitPlanner today.
