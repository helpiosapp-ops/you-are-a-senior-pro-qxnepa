
# Privacy Policy for ExitPlanner

**Last Updated:** February 24, 2025

## Overview

ExitPlanner is a privacy-first application designed to help professionals prepare for career transitions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete data security.

## Data Collection

**We collect ZERO data.**

ExitPlanner does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Track your usage or behavior
- Store data on external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Share data with third parties
- Require user accounts or registration
- Access your contacts, photos, or other device data

## Data Storage

All data you create in ExitPlanner is stored **locally on your device only**. This includes:
- Your selected exit path
- Checklist progress
- Personal notes
- Any other information you enter

Your data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not synced to the cloud
- Is not backed up to our servers (because we don't have any)
- Remains completely private and under your control

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:
- Uninstalling the app from your device
- Clearing the app's data through your device settings

Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered because we don't store it anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

ExitPlanner does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

ExitPlanner does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices (which would only happen if we add features that require it), we will update this policy and notify users through the app.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@exitplanner.app

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to:
- Request access to
- Request deletion of
- Request correction of
- Request portability of

Your data is yours, stored only on your device, and completely private.

---

**Summary:** ExitPlanner is a completely offline, privacy-first app. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. Everything stays on your device.
